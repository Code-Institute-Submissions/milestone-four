Lobke van Aar

Lobke van Aar (1982) - working in the fields of illustration and custom lettering - is currently living and working in Rotterdam, in the Netherlands. Since 2008 she has been working as a freelancer on a daily basis.

The works by Lobke van Aar are a playful mix of illustration, color and typography. Lobke has a passion for hand lettering, a fascination for the whispers of the past and is always looking for the human element in her topics. In her day to day life it's the little things that jump out and inspire her.
